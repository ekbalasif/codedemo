SPONSORS

Sponsorship Options

Increase your visibility and your organization's profile by becoming a sponsor for BTAS 2019. BTAS 2019 will be co-located with the FedID -- the U.S. federal government’s primary outreach and collaboration-building event in the worldwide identity community since 1995. Please see below for more information about sponsorship options.

Sponsorship Benifits

  1. Platinum Sponsor($10,000): Free admission for up to 4 attendees Sponsorship of doctoral consortium or Best Paper Award All benefits in the “silver” category
  2. Gold Sponsor($5,000): Free admission for up to 2 attendees Sponsorship of Best Student Paper Award or Best Reviewer Award All benefits in the “silver” category
  3. Silver Sponsor($2,500): Logo on posters in the conference venue Logo on the program booklet Poster space in conference area Publicity at BTAS welcome reception and at banquet Five-minute pitch of company products or recruitment at banquet

How to Sponsor BTAS 2019

To arrange sponsorship of your company for BTAS 2019 or discuss custom packages please contact the sponsorship chairs Vitomir Štruc and Adam Czajka.
